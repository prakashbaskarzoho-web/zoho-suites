Mail

Zoho Mail is a secure, ad-free email suite for businesses with custom domains, advanced admin controls, and integrated calendar, contacts, and tasks.

Collaborate with Streams, share drafts, and manage conversations efficiently with clean, modern apps across devices.

Key Features

  • Custom domain email: Professional mailboxes with aliases and group addresses.
  • Built-in productivity: Calendar, contacts, tasks, and notes tightly integrated.
  • Streams collaboration: Comment, tag, and share emails without forwards.
  • Security and admin: Spam protection, encryption, and detailed policies.
  • Cross-platform apps: Web, mobile, and desktop access with consistent UX.
