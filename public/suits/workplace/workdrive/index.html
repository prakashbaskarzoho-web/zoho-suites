WorkDrive

Zoho WorkDrive is a secure team file management platform to store, share, and collaborate on documents, spreadsheets, and presentations.

Organize content with Team Folders, govern access with granular permissions, and track activity with detailed insights.

Key Features

  • Team Folders: Structure content by department or project with access controls.
  • Secure sharing: Public links with passwords, expiry, and download restrictions.
  • Office suite collaboration: Work together in Writer, Sheet, and Show in real time.
  • External collaboration: Share files with clients while retaining data controls.
  • Admin console and insights: Audit trails, storage metrics, and device management.
