CRM

Zoho CRM Plus is a unified customer experience platform that helps you manage leads, deals, support, marketing, and analytics across the customer lifecycle—all in one place.

Streamline processes with automation, gain visibility with dashboards, and deliver personalized experiences across channels.

Key Features

  • Lead and deal management: Capture, qualify, and track deals with customizable pipelines.
  • Omnichannel engagement: Connect via email, phone, chat, social, and portals from a single console.
  • Automation and workflows: Build workflows, assignment rules, and blueprints to reduce manual work.
  • Analytics and dashboards: Monitor KPIs with prebuilt and custom reports and dashboards.
  • Ecosystem integrations: Connect with Zoho apps and third-party services to extend capabilities.
