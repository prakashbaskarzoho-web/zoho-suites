# Cliq
Cliq is an all-in-one digital workplace that combines chat, video conferencing, file sharing, and management tools to help teams collaborate and work together more efficiently.

With Cliq, you can create channels for different projects, topics or teams, and invite team members to join. You can share files, make video or audio calls, and send messages to team members. Cliq also has a built-in task manager that allows you to assign tasks to team members and track progress.

## Key Features

* **Channels**: Create channels for different projects, topics or teams, and invite team members to join.
* **Video and audio conferencing**: Hold video or audio conferences with team members from anywhere in the world.
* **File sharing**: Share files with team members and collaborate on projects.
* **Task management**: Assign tasks to team members and track progress.
* **Integration**: Integrate with other Zoho apps such as CRM, Mail, and Office to enhance your workflow.
