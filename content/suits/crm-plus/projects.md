# Projects
Zoho Projects is an end-to-end project management solution to plan, track, and deliver work on time. Organize tasks, collaborate with teams, and visualize progress with powerful charts and reports.

Keep stakeholders aligned with shared timelines, automate routine work, and monitor workload to optimize team capacity.

## Key Features

* **Task and subtask management**: Break down work, set priorities, and track progress with Kanban and list views.
* **Gantt charts and timelines**: Visualize schedules, dependencies, and critical paths to avoid delays.
* **Time tracking**: Log billable/non-billable hours and generate timesheets for invoicing.
* **Issue tracking**: Capture, assign, and resolve bugs or risks with SLAs and custom fields.
* **Automation and integrations**: Set up blueprints, reminders, and connect with Zoho and third-party apps.
