# Meeting
Zoho Meeting is secure online meeting and webinar software for scheduling, hosting, and recording sessions with teams and customers.

Share screens, manage participants with moderator controls, and engage audiences with polls, Q&A, and registrations.

## Key Features

* **HD meetings and webinars**: Host secure meetings with no downloads for attendees.
* **Screen sharing and recording**: Present and record sessions to share later.
* **Moderator controls**: Lock meetings, mute participants, and manage permissions.
* **Engagement tools**: Polls, Q&A, hand raise, and registration workflows.
* **Integrations**: Connect with Zoho apps and calendars for smooth scheduling.
