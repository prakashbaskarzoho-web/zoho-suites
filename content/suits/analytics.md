# Analytics
Analytics is a customer relationship management (CRM) software that helps businesses manage their customer interactions and relationships.

With powerful data connectors, data preparation, and AI-assisted analytics, you can bring data from multiple sources, blend it, and uncover trends through rich visualizations and sharing options.

## Key Features

* **Wide data connectors**: Import data from files, databases, cloud apps, and custom APIs to create a unified view.
* **Data preparation**: Cleanse, transform, and blend datasets using a visual, no-code interface.
* **Interactive dashboards**: Build and share drill-down dashboards with filters, widgets, and embedded analytics.
* **AI insights (Zia)**: Ask questions in natural language and get instant reports and explanations.
* **Collaboration and sharing**: Schedule reports, share securely with teams, and control access with granular permissions.
