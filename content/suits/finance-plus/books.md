# Books
Zoho Books is online accounting software to manage invoicing, expenses, banking, inventory, and financial reporting—helping businesses stay compliant and cash-flow positive.

Stay on top of receivables and payables, automate routine accounting, and collaborate with your accountant securely in the cloud.

## Key Features

* **Professional invoicing**: Create branded invoices, automate reminders, and accept online payments.
* **Expenses and bills**: Capture expenses, manage vendor bills, and track reimbursements.
* **Banking and reconciliation**: Connect bank feeds and reconcile transactions quickly.
* **Inventory tracking**: Manage items, stock levels, and price lists.
* **Tax compliance and reports**: Generate GST/VAT-ready returns and financial statements.
* **Client portal**: Share quotes, invoices, and get paid faster with a self-service portal.
