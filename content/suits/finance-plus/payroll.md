# Payroll
Zoho Payroll automates salary calculations, tax deductions, and payslips so you can run payroll accurately and on time every month.

Stay compliant with local statutory rules, manage reimbursements, bonuses, and maintain secure employee records.

## Key Features

* **Automated payroll runs**: Configure earnings, deductions, and payroll schedules.
* **Statutory compliance**: Handle taxes, PF/ESI, and form filings as applicable.
* **Direct deposit and payslips**: Pay employees and share digital payslips securely.
* **Leave and attendance**: Integrate time off and attendance for accurate payroll.
* **Employee self-service**: Let employees view payslips, submit proofs, and update info.
